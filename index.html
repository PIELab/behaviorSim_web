BehaviorSim dynamical modeling toolkit for behavioral science

BehaviorSim tools help researchers formulate and test models using simulations, guide experimental design and theory development. The before/after section below contrasts methods with and without behaviorSim.
Planning an Experiment and Modeling your Expectation
With a research question in mind, scientists work to choose the right behavioral theory and then must think through how it applies to the case in question. Predictions from the chosen theory are estimated qualitatively by the researcher, who must think through the psychological processes of the subject as they go through the experiment.
