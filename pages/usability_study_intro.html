BehaviorSim dynamical modeling toolkit for behavioral science

This study is designed to assess the usability of the behaviorSim Model Building Tool. This tool has three aims:
  1. Improve user's usage and understanding of system dynamics.
  2. Make formulation of dynamical models of human behavior more accessible.
  3. Enable sharing and extending of existing models.
There are four tasks we are asking you to complete:
  1. Pre-test to gauge your understanding of system dynamics.
  2. Attempt to build a model which we describe to you using the BehaviorSim Model Building Tool.
  3. User experience survey for you to provide your opinion of the tool.
  4. User design feedback questionnaire for your to provide design feedback & suggestions.
  5. Post-test to gauge your understanding of system dynamics.
All tasks are optional. You are free to skip any question or task and to quit or stop at any time.
